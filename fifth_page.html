Khvicha Kvaratskhelia named 2022/23 UEFA Champions League
Young Player of the Season

Sunday, June 11, 2023

The Napoli winger takes the award after a dazzling breakthrough season in the competition.

Khvicha Kvaratskhelia - Full Season Show

UEFA's Technical Observer panel have named Napoli's Khvicha Kvaratskhelia as the 2022/23 UEFA Champions League Young
Player of the Season.

Signed in July 2022 from Dinamo Batumi in his native Georgia, the 22-year-old left winger turned heads as his side reached the
quarter-finals of Europe's top club competition, losing out to domestic rivals AC Milan.

Napoli boss Luciano Spalletti said of him: "Kvara is gorgeous, magnificent and delightful. A top player, despite his age. He still
has a lot to learn and when he does he will become deadly, a super footballer."

Kvaratskhelia's first season in Serie A was stunning too; he helped Napoli to win their first championship since 1990 and was
named as the league's Player of the Season. So impressive were his performances that he has been nicknamed 'Kvaradona', a
nod to Napoli's Argentinian great Diego Maradona.

Kvaratskhelia's 2022/23 Champions League stats

Appearances: 9
Goals: 2
Assists: 4
Passing accuracy: 74.23%
Distance covered: 76.32km
Top speed: 33.6km/h
